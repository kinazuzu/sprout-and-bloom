Description

Golden Pathos is one of the easiest houseplants to grow. Its heart-shaped leaves with yellow-green variegation make it a favorite for homes, offices, and study areas. It thrives indoors and only needs simple care to stay healthy and beautiful.

Plant Care

  • Water once a week or when soil feels dry
  • Place in bright, indirect sunlight
  • Use well-draining soil
  • Fertilize once a month during growing season

Benefits

  • Air-purifying plant
  • Beginner-friendly
  • Low maintenance
  • Great for indoor decoration
